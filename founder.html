Biography

Born: 13 October 2002, Ankara

Medine Ak is a Turkish entrepreneur and beauty specialist merging AI, fashion and aesthetics. Alongside her education, she has earned five certifications in advanced entrepreneurship and beauty, establishing herself as a disciplined, visionary young leader shaping the global fashion-tech scene.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To build Turkey’s first AI-powered fashion-tech ecosystem and expand it globally, inspiring women to shine like stars in every aspect of life.

Mission

To empower individuals through technology and aesthetics, creating a space where style meets innovation and every woman finds her unique brilliance.

Values & Inspiration

Inspiration

Medine’s journey reflects her belief that true power comes from growth, independence, and authenticity — transforming challenges into elegance.

Core Values

Originality, boldness, elegance, and continuous innovation define her leadership and creative philosophy.

Future Goals

2025–2026

Launch MedaStaré globally and build a strong international user base.

2027

Expand into fashion-tech markets with AI-powered styling and personalized ecosystems.

2030

Establish MedaStaré as a world-class brand with flagship showrooms in Paris, Milan, and Dubai.
