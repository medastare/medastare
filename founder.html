Founder & CEO – Medine Ak

Medine Ak

Visionary Entrepreneur shaping the future of fashion-tech

Biography

Born: 13 October 2002, Ankara

Medine Ak is a young Turkish entrepreneur and beauty specialist who combines AI, fashion and aesthetics. Alongside her education, she has earned 5 certifications in advanced entrepreneurship and beauty, establishing herself as a determined and disciplined leader.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To build Turkey’s first AI-powered fashion-tech ecosystem and expand it globally, inspiring women to shine like stars in every aspect of life.

Mission

Empowering individuals through technology and aesthetics, creating a platform where style meets innovation, and every woman finds her unique brilliance.

Values & Inspiration

Inspiration

Medine takes inspiration from her own journey, proving that true power comes from self-growth and celebrating personal achievements.

Core Values

Originality, boldness, elegance, and continuous innovation define her work and leadership style.

Future Goals

2025–2026

Launch MedaStaré to global audiences and secure a strong international user base.

2027

Expand into global fashion-tech markets with AI-powered styling tools and product lines.

2030

Establish MedaStaré as a worldwide brand and open flagship showrooms in fashion capitals.
